Welcome

Welcome to my travel page! Explore one of the Philippines' most beloved destinations:"Baguio" City, famously known as the "City of Pines." This guide will walk you through its must-see attractions, delicious local food, and practical tips to help you plan your perfect getaway. Whether you're here for nature, culture, or great food, Baguio offers an unforgettable experience!

Top Attractions

Baguio is home to some of the most scenic and historic spots in the Philippines. Here are a few must-visit places:

  • Burnham Park – A relaxing green space perfect for boating, biking, and leisurely strolls.
  • Mines View Park – Offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys, and a great spot for souvenir shopping.
  • Camp John Hay – A former American military base, now a leisure and eco-tourism park, with activities ranging from hiking.
  • Botanical Garden – A lush, tranquil space filled with native plants, perfect for nature lovers and photographers.

Famous Local Food

Baguio is a food lover's paradise, known for its fresh produce and unique flavors. Don't miss out on these iconic local dishes and treats:

  • Strawberries – Sweet and juicy strawberries, often enjoyed fresh or as part of desserts like strawberry shortcake.
  • Ube Jam – A rich, sweet purple yam spread that is a local delicacy, perfect as a souvenir or to enjoy on toast.
  • Fresh Vegetables – Baguio is famous for its cool climate, which makes it ideal for growing fresh, crisp veggies like lettuce, carrots, and beans.

To taste these local delights, be sure to visit the Baguio Public Market and the lively night markets, where you'll find street food vendors serving up fresh, affordable treats!
