Top Attractions

Baguio is home to some of the most scenic and historic spots in the Philippines. Here are a few must-visit places:

  • Burnham Park – A relaxing green space perfect for boating, biking, and leisurely strolls.
  • Mines View Park – Offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys, and a great spot for souvenir shopping.
  • Camp John Hay – A former American military base, now a leisure and eco-tourism park, with activities ranging from hiking.
  • Botanical Garden – A lush, tranquil space filled with native plants, perfect for nature lovers and photographers.
